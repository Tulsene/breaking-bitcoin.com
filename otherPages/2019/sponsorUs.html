The Events

Breaking Bitcoin was born at the climax of the Scaling Debate. We, as a community, had enough of this focus on “scaling” when the real fundamentals are still under-researched. Privacy. Censorship-resistance. Decentralization. All for one ultimate goal: an extremely secure and resilient system to serve as the foundations of future infrastructure, services and products.

Breaking Bitcoin Vol. I (Paris, 2017) brought together 300 attendees from all over the world, for a world-class conference about Bitcoin protocol security. Let’s do even better this year!

For many attendees, Breaking Bitcoin is a rare if not the only opportunity during the year to meet and exchange with so many core members of the Bitcoin community.

To increase the value for every participants, we are adding side events during the week following the conference:

  • 7th June: Pre-registration and social meetup
  • 8th and 9th June: Breaking Bitcoin Vol. II (up to 500 participants)
  • 11th to 13th June: a Bitcoin technical training over 3 days

The Conference


The Talks

Breaking Bitcoin Vol. II will host the world-best speakers on their respective area of expertise, to challenge the Bitcoin security model.

Hardware security, Governance, Protocol-level architecture, Social attacks, from both the theoretical and practical perspectives.

Breaking Bitcoin is aimed at Bitcoin professionals, academics and enthusiasts. Attendees are expected to be familiar and up to date with Bitcoin’s state of the art as you can see here

As usual, the talks will be live-streamed and recorded.


In Details

Breaking Bitcoin Vol. II will be hosted in Amsterdam, in the famous conference center Beurs Van Berlage. As previously, the quality of the event is a priority and will reflect on your brand should you decide to sponsor this community conference.

  • 20+ world-class experts giving a talk, hundreds in attendance
  • Up to 500 attendees from the Bitcoin technical community
  • 2 full days of talks and panels, networking evenings and a week of satellite events, to offer a unique BitcoinTech Week
  • Live-streamed and Recorded
  • No bullshit, no ICO, no scams
  • By the community, for the community. Very affordable tickets starting at 250 EUR, 50€ for students.

The Training

Training Page

As it is more and more important to train new developers to Bitcoin engineering and development, Breaking Bitcoin is now offering an intensive training for different skill levels. Inspired by the successful Bitcoin Edge from the Scaling Bitcoin conference, this applied course will take place right after the Breaking Bitcoin Vol. II conference.

  • 3 days of lectures and workshops
  • a handful of lecturers and assistants
  • 50 participants expected
  • 300 EUR tickets including lunches
